About Us

Welcome to The Lift Coffee Shop, where every cup tells a story! Nestled in an idyllic spot just off the San Juan River near Cotton Hole, and steps away from the scenic walking path, our café is the perfect place to start off your "Pa-go-slow" vacation with a caffeinated kick.

Owned and operated by Dali and Miro, two self-proclaimed coffee fanatics, The Lift has quickly become a local favorite. Their passion for the perfect cup of joe is as strong as the coffee they brew. Whether you're a local or just passing through, Dali and Miro are dedicated to making sure your coffee experience is one you won't soon forget. Come for the coffee, stay for the vibe—The Lift is where your perfect cup awaits!
